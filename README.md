# Research Funding Opportunity Intelligence & Grant Management System

## Project Overview

This project simulates the workflow of a Research Development and Grants Management Office responsible for tracking funding opportunities, supporting researchers, and monitoring grant portfolios.

The system manages research funding data from national and international agencies including DBT, DST, BIRAC, ICMR, CSIR, ANRF, NIH, WHO, and Gates Foundation.

## Key Features

* Grant Opportunity Database
* Funding Agency Analysis
* Research Area Analysis
* Faculty-Grant Matching
* Deadline Risk Monitoring
* Executive KPI Reporting
* Excel-Based Funding Intelligence Report
* SQLite Database Management

## Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* SQLite
* SQL
* Matplotlib
* Excel

## Business Impact

The project demonstrates how research administration teams can identify funding opportunities, monitor grant deadlines, analyze funding portfolios, and support faculty members in securing research funding.

## Dataset

* 150 Grant Opportunities
* 9 Funding Agencies
* 10 Research Domains
* Automated KPI Reporting

## Deliverables

* Funding Opportunity Database
* Executive Summary Report
* Funding Intelligence Dashboard
* Faculty Eligibility Matching System
* Deadline Monitoring System
